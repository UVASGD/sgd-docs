Branding for SGD at UVA

Some general guidelines follow: * SGD at UVA must always be presend as SGD at UVA or Student Game Developers at the University of Virginia or some derivative. * SGD at UVA may not be refered to as UVA SGD for any official purposes. In order to do this, SGD at UVA will have to achieve special status with the university. * All uses of any SGD at UVA branding must support one of the following logos in a recognizable context.
